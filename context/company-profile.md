# Company Profile

**Company Name**: TechFlow SaaS Solutions
**Industry**: B2B Software-as-a-Service (SaaS)
**Founded**: 2023
**Team Size**: 15 employees
**Target Market**: Small to medium businesses (SMBs)

## Product Overview

TechFlow provides a unified workflow automation platform that helps businesses:
- Automate repetitive tasks across multiple applications
- Integrate disparate business tools
- Streamline team collaboration
- Reduce manual data entry by 80%

## Pricing Tiers

| Plan | Monthly Price | Features |
|------|---------------|----------|
| Starter | $29/month | Up to 5 users, 100 automations, basic integrations |
| Professional | $79/month | Up to 20 users, 500 automations, advanced integrations, priority support |
| Enterprise | Custom | Unlimited users, unlimited automations, custom integrations, dedicated support |
